About

I’m an anesthesiologist and faculty member at Iran University of Medical Sciences (IUMS). My work bridges clinical anesthesiology, enhanced recovery (ERAS), and applied machine learning for medical imaging and perioperative decision support.

I collaborate with residents and trainees on projects in ML‑guided hemodynamics, opioid‑sparing analgesia, and robust CMR image reconstruction.

Research

CMR Reconstruction
AdaptCMR: spectrally guided mixture‑of‑experts for efficient multi‑contrast reconstruction. Parameter‑efficient decoders with view/contrast specialization.
Perioperative ML
Predictive modeling for hypotension, opioid‑sparing analgesia, and ML‑guided decision support in ERAS pathways.
IQA & Domain Adaptation
Robust CMR IQA and adaptation from simulated to real‑world artifacts (motion, Gibbs, aliasing).
Methodology
Mixture‑of‑experts routing, spectral guidance, prompt‑style conditioning, and physics‑guided unrolled baselines.
