About

I am an anesthesiologist and faculty member at Iran University of Medical Sciences (IUMS). My work spans perioperative care, enhanced recovery, and applied machine learning for medical imaging—with a focus on cardiac MRI reconstruction, image quality assessment, and ML-guided decision support.

Interests:
Anesthesiology Perioperative AI Cardiac MRI Domain Adaptation Mixture-of-Experts Image Reconstruction

Research

AdaptCMR: Spectrally guided mixture-of-experts for efficient multi-contrast CMR reconstruction.
ML-Guided Anesthesiology: Predictive modeling for hypotension, opioid-sparing analgesia, and ERAS pathways.
Image Quality & Domain Adaptation: Robust CMR IQA and generalization to real-world artefacts.
